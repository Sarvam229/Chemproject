Cough and Cold Medicines

Medicine-1

1. PRODUCT INTRODUCTION
Sinarest Tablet is used to treat symptoms of the common cold and allergies like sneezing, runny/stuffy nose, fever, headache, body pains, congestion, or watery eyes.
The common cold is a respiratory illness affecting the nose and throat. It is mostly caused by viruses known as 'rhinovirus'. Acetaminophen is a pain reliever and fever reducer.

2. SALT COMPOSITION
Chlorpheniramine Maleate (2mg) + Paracetamol (500mg) + Phenylephrine (10mg)

3. COMMON SIDE EFFECTS OF SINAREST
  • Dizziness
  • Nausea
  • Headache
  • Insomnia (difficulty in sleeping)
  • Tachycardia
  • Palpitations
  • Fear
  • Anxiety
  • Restlessness
  • Tremors
  • Weakness
4. HOW TO USE SINAREST TABLET
Take this medicine in the dose and duration as advised by your doctor. Swallow it as a whole. Do not chew, crush or break it.
Sinarest New Tablet may be taken with or without food, but it is better to take it at a fixed time.

5. SAFETY ADVICE
  • Alcohol
  • Pregnancy
  • Breast feeding
  • Driving
  • Kidney
  • Liver
6. WHAT IF YOU FORGET TO TAKE SINAREST TABLET?
If you miss a dose of Sinarest New Tablet, take it as soon as possible. However, if it is almost time for your next dose,
skip the missed dose and go back to your regular schedule. Do not double the dose.
